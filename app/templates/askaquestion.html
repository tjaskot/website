So!!! What is the purpose of this site? Great question!

Site is to create a centralized location of knowledge I've gathered amongst community and in professional scenarios so others can leverage it freely and openly. To trigger thoughts and inspire research driven ideation. To help me make a mark on learning something new. Only further proving that more a person learns, the more they don't know, and open another dimension of infinite knowledge only to find learning is the best part.

Hence, why writing it down in a fashion more enjoyable and digestible than a blank slate of monotonous toned Resume summaries is not designed for such, rather in hope to spread and share learned knowledge with others, so they can learn even quicker and build even more!

Lastly, it puts myself on digital paper. People can read a resume, great. But that's not me, I'd rather show you. We all believe we're different, AND WE ARE, but we never really get a chance to show it, or for that matter, never have a chance to shine. I found I can do that through visuals and code, and side note, why I venerate Photoshop for its ability to manipulate a moment in time.
