TNFJ (TJ 'N Friends)

Quick Overview: TNFJ is for purpose of building community development and sharing best practices across our current ecosystem. As the owner, Trevor, I am very passionate about building people around us. Because our world is our influence :)

The consulting portion of my business centers itself around work where knowledge and experience interweave to create the best possible outcomes with information at that time. Not everything needs to be suave and debonair, but it does have the ability to be its best when the provider puts passion and effort into the product. A quote which rang true for me is: "a person who wants to walk will always go further than a person who walks to the finish."

That's why I created my own business. So many people have such wealth of knowledge and diversity of thought, but why don't we leverage this? It's not because we don't have access to it, in my opinion, it's because we don't know where to go to start looking. However, the beauty of tenacity and persistence is that any outcome can be achieved when one walks to learn, not walks to gain.

That's my hope with this company. To start something that others feel empowered by and strive to do the same that I've done. Reach for the stars, because even a fall will land you at the top of the trees.

So!!! What is the purpose of this site? Great question!

Site is to create a centralized location of knowledge I've gathered amongst community and in professional scenarios so others can leverage it freely and openly. To trigger thoughts and inspire research driven ideation. To help me make a mark on learning something new. Only further proving that more a person learns, the more they don't know, and open another dimension of infinite knowledge only to find learning is the best part. Hence, why writing it down in a fashion more enjoyable and digestible than a blank slate of monotonous toned Resume summaries is not designed for such, rather in hope to spread and share learned knowledge with others, so they can learn even quicker and build even more! Lastly, it puts myself on digital paper. People can read a resume, great. But that's not me, I'd rather show you. We all believe we're different, AND WE ARE, but we never really get a chance to show it, or for that matter, never have a chance to shine. I found I can do that through visuals and code, and side note, why I venerate Photoshop for its ability to manipulate a moment in time.
